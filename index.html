About the Meet

One evening. One family.

A warm gathering for the entire LEAMS family — friends, faculty, alumni, and loved ones — coming together to celebrate, reconnect, and create memories that stay.

01 / Verified

ID-Verified Entry

Every guest is verified through a government-issued ID for the safety and comfort of our LEAMS family.

02 / Single Pass

One Person · One Ticket

To keep the gathering intimate and well-organised, we issue exactly one pass per individual. Non-transferable.

03 / Free

Complimentary

Entry to the Family Meet is free. Just register, verify, and download your pass — we'll see you there.
